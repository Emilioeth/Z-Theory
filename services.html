THE STEPS

Discovery
Getting to know our clients and understanding them is perhaphs the most important part of the process, which is why the first step we take when considering working with a new client, is dive in and research your business, competition, and your market. We will then schedule a Zoom call to go over your goals and what you're expecting to gain from a partnership with us. And if we both think we would be a good match we will make you an offer.
Strategy
The strategy or planning stage is the second step. At this stage we already have a basic understanding of your business from our past research and meetings with you, we will build a strategy that specifically aligns to your goals, this step normally includes retargeting, reviewing your past ad campaigns if you have any, and making adjustments based on past results. This of course depends on the client, some clients already have ad campaigns running that they just want to improve and some don't, whichever the case might be we are ready to help take care of your marketing needs while you take care of other aspects of your business.
Application
At this stage we will begin to create content to advertise for your business based on the strategy we tailored made for you, taking into consideration target audience, product being advertised, and the chosen platform, we will carefully design the content and advertise it to your target audience. Our approach to business is a very efficient but meticulate one, while we are very design centered, our main focus remains on results, and at the end of the day our main goal is to get our clients a great ROI.
Results
The last step are the results, we believe in communication, we think of our clients as an important relationship in our business, which is why we think it is crucial to be transparent and communicative. We give our clients a weekly or bi-weekly update on how they're ads are doing, included with our suggestions. We are constantly shifting our strategy to better accomodate to the current market and maximize results, we do not like to settle, if we are making you a 2x ROI we believe we could be making you a 4x. Which is why 90% of the time we are adjusting our approach depending on our analysis of the past month. Keep in mind a lot of the times worthy results can take more than a month, especially for businesses that havent been advertising in the chosen platform.
