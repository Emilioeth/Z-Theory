THE STEPS

Discovery
Getting to know our clients and understanding them is perhaphs the most important part of the process, which is why the first step we take when working together is dive in and truly understand what our clients goals are. We analyze our client and create a psychological profile, we identify what they like, what they dont, what they are focused on and how we could help. We truly try to make our clients feel as involved in the process as possible, sometimes they have ideas that just work and other times the ideas don't work.
Concept
The concept or planning stage is the second step. At this stage we begin reasearching and brainstorming, sometimes the client knows exactly what they want and sometimes they don't, so depending on that this stage could be a longer one, or a very short one. By the end of this stage we would have a plan, or concept, that best meets our clients needs, at which point we would receive feedback from the client and make adjustments accordingly.
Design
When the client is happy with the concept the next stage begins. At this point we will start working on the design of the project, this mostly means wireframing and illustration. At this point making changes to the concept can be very problematic, which is why we always like to make sure the concept is final and fully approved.
Development
The last step is the development or implementation. At this stage we have a fully painted picture in our heads of how the project should look, and so we start developing it. We always like to make sure that the client feels satisfied and happy with our service, which is why we always like to make sure the final product is on par or above with the clients standards.

Why Us ?

We strive for greatness, we like to make memorable work, not only work that our clients are proud of, but that we are proud of. We believe in our work doing the talking.
